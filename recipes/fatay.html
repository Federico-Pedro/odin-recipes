Fatay

Fatay is the argentinian name given to this spectacular lebanese pie

The best recipes come from the fusion of diverse cultures

Ingredients:

  • 24 empanada dough circles
  • 1 kg grounded meat
  • 2 big onions
  • 2 big tomatoes
  • Baharat
  • Salt
  • Lemmon juice
  • Green onion (optional)

Steps:

  1. Cut al the vegetables in brunoise.
  2. In a big bowl mix the meat with the vegetables, and the rest of the ingredients.
  3. Let it rest in the fridge for 30 minutes.
  4. Take a ball of the stuff and fill a circle with it, close it in a triangle shape.
  5. Cook them in a preheated oven, high temperature for 30-40 minutes.
  6. You can add a few drops of lemon or some taratur sauce.Enjoy!
